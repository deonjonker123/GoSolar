# GoSolar

A lightweight, no-nonsense RF solar generation and storage mod for Minecraft 1.21.1 (NeoForge).

No multiblocks. No complex machines. No inverters. Just panels, batteries, and power.

---

## Features

- Five tiers of solar panels, from basic early-game generation to serious endgame power
- Five tiers of batteries with built-in item charging slot

---

## Blocks

### Solar Panels

Solar panels generate RF when exposed to daylight. Generation stops at night and during rain.

| Panel | Generation | Transfer Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Basic Solar Panel | 128 RF/t | 1,024 RF/t |
| Hardened Solar Panel | 512 RF/t | 4,096 RF/t |
| Advanced Solar Panel | 2,048 RF/t | 16,384 RF/t |
| Elite Solar Panel | 8,192 RF/t | 65,536 RF/t |
| Ultimate Solar Panel | 32,768 RF/t | 262,144 RF/t |

### Batteries

Batteries store RF and accept powered items in their charging slot.

| Battery | Capacity | Transfer Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Basic Battery | 2,000,000 RF | 1,024 RF/t |
| Hardened Battery | 8,000,000 RF | 4,096 RF/t |
| Advanced Battery | 32,000,000 RF | 16,384 RF/t |
| Elite Battery | 64,000,000 RF | 65,536 RF/t |
| Ultimate Battery | 128,000,000 RF | 262,144 RF/t |
